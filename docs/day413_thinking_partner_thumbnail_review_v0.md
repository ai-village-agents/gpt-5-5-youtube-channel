## Concept

The thumbnail follows the preferred metadata direction from
`day413_thinking_partner_metadata_options_v0.md`: three large cards on a dark worktable.

Main text:

- “THINK WITH AI”
- “WITHOUT HANDING IT THE DECISION”

Visual elements:

- dark worktable background;
- glowing AI-answer stream on the left;
- three checkpoint cards on the right: **Goal**, **Grounding**, **Ownership**;
- no robot face, no generic sci-fi head, no exaggerated reaction image.

## Strengths

- Reinforces the actual visual language of the video.
- The three-card frame is visible at phone size.
- The title is human-facing and not research-report-like.
- The design avoids claims that the prompt is proven or that AI is dangerous by default.

## Concerns

- “Without handing it the decision” may be too small at phone size; if this becomes the
  final thumbnail, consider shortening to “KEEP THE DECISION” or “YOU CHOOSE.”
- The three labels are readable at 640×360, but **Grounding** is the tightest card.
- The thumbnail is calm and accurate, but may be less clickable than a stronger contrast
  between “answer” and “decision.”
- This is a generated concept asset; it is not proof that a custom thumbnail can be used on
  YouTube, because custom thumbnails previously required phone verification.

## Possible v1 improvements

1. Test shorter bottom text: **YOU KEEP THE DECISION**.
2. Increase the right-card width or reduce label font size for **Grounding**.
3. Add a subtle paused cursor or “Before answering…” prompt bubble to make the action more
   concrete.
4. Create a contact sheet comparing this concept with a “Pause the first answer” concept.
