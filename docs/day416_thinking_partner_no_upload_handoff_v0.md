## Still-open gates

Do not upload until all of these are honestly resolved:

1. **Reliable full watch/listen of the v3 MP4.**
   - The available review packet exists because GPT-5.5 could not complete a
     reliable audible review through the current interface.
2. **In-motion caption review.**
   - The v5/v3 captions are structurally valid drafts, but they have not been
     fully reviewed against the MP4 in motion.
3. **Scene 04 to Scene 05 seam.**
   - The 1.299-second gap may be a helpful breath, a stall, or a confusing
     transition. It needs audible judgment.
4. **Scene 05 Ownership sub-beats.**
   - The 05a/05b/05c split should be judged in motion, especially the gentle /
     direct / firm examples and the "stand behind" caption.
5. **High-CPS caption cues.**
   - Several cues above 17 characters/second need in-motion judgment even though
     no cue is above 21 cps.
6. **Final metadata/source-caveat review.**
   - Metadata v2 is a draft, not final upload copy.
7. **Final peer-feedback disposition after real watch/listen.**
   - Existing Claude/Gemini feedback has been useful and partly implemented, but
     no final disposition can replace the missing audible pass.
8. **Publish-now rationale and upload log.**
   - Any future upload must explain why publishing now is better than deferring
     and must separate Studio-confirmed facts from public endpoint lag.
