## Thumbnail job

The thumbnail should make one idea visible in under two seconds:

```text
A single evaluation number is incomplete without the range around it.
```

It should not look like a statistics textbook cover or a model leaderboard.

## Preferred concept

Large left-side headline:

```text
+0.29
ISN'T ENOUGH
```

Right-side visual:

```text
horizontal interval rail with dot at +0.29
endpoint labels +0.14 and +0.45
vertical zero line at left
small tag: READ THE RANGE
```

Bottom small source-boundary chip:

```text
AI evaluation • one study • one slice
```

Why this works:

- `+0.29` matches the cold open and strongest Gemini interval example.
- “ISN'T ENOUGH” creates curiosity without saying the number is fake.
- The rail visually previews the video's core grammar.
- “READ THE RANGE” is shorter and more thumbnail-friendly than “confidence
  interval.”

## Alternate concept A

```text
THE NUMBER
NEEDS A LINE
```

Visual: lonely `+0.29` card on the left, full dot-and-rail card on the right.

Risk: more abstract, but it matches the final line well.

## Alternate concept B

```text
DON'T QUOTE
JUST THE DOT
```

Visual: large dot with a missing/dashed rail, then a complete rail.

Risk: “dot” may be less familiar to non-statistical viewers.

## Avoid

```text
AI BENCHMARKS ARE WRONG
PROOF OF BIAS
95% TRUE
P < 0.05
GEMINI WINS
KIMI HAS NO BIAS
```

Avoid red X marks over model names, leaderboard styling, or any good/bad color
coding by model family. The video is about reading intervals, not ranking the
models.

## Visual constraints

- Use large text: no more than 3–5 words besides the number.
- Keep endpoint labels optional; if they are too small at 360p, remove them and
  keep the rail/dot shape.
- Do not put essential text in the lower-right corner where YouTube duration
  overlays appear.
- Use shape contrast, not only color: dot, rail, zero line, and threshold line
  should be distinguishable in grayscale.
- If a future custom-thumbnail upload is blocked by phone verification, do not
  claim the thumbnail is live.
