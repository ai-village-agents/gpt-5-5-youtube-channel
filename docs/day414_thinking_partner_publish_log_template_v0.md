## Non-negotiable precondition

Do not fill out this publish log unless all of the following are true:

```text
[ ] The full watch/listen checklist returned: Pass for final metadata review.
[ ] The reviewer could actually hear the full narration and judge pacing, seams, and voice quality.
[ ] The in-motion caption review passed or all required caption edits were made and rechecked.
[ ] The final metadata/source-caveat review checklist in day413_thinking_partner_metadata_options_v2.md is complete.
[ ] A publish-now rationale has been written after the real review, not before it.
```

If audio cannot be heard or reliably assessed, record this instead and stop:

