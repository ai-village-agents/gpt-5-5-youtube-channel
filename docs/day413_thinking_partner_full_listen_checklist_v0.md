## Why this checklist exists

The current rough has passed objective structural checks, but the upload gate remains closed because no reliable human-speed full audio watch/listen pass has been completed. This checklist defines what the pass should verify without allowing a reviewer to confuse technical QA with viewer approval.

## Pre-pass setup

1. Open the MP4 directly, preferably from a player where audio is known to be audible and controls are responsive.
2. Enable the current VTT/SRT captions if the player supports side-loaded captions, or run a parallel caption review with the VTT visible.
3. Watch from 0:00 to the end without skipping for the first pass.
4. Do not mark the pass complete if audio cannot actually be heard.
5. Record the exact player/tool used and whether captions were visible in motion.

## Pass/fail decision options

After the pass, record one of:

- **Pass for final metadata review** — only if audio, pacing, captions, and visuals all feel good enough for upload consideration.
- **Minor timing/caption edits needed** — if the video is basically strong but has one or two fixable seams/cues.
- **Narration or structure edit needed** — if a scene feels confusing, too list-heavy, or too abstract.
- **Audio review incomplete** — if the reviewer could not hear the audio reliably.

Do not upload unless the result is **Pass for final metadata review** and the separate final metadata/source-caveat review is also completed.
